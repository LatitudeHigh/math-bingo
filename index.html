Math Bingo!

How To Play

If you know how to play bingo, knowing how to play Math Bingo should be easy. There is a box filled with different colored cirlces that each display how easy or how hard the problem is, a red circle is a hard math question, a yellow circle is a intermediate math question, and a green circle is a easy math question. When a circle is clicked a math question will be displayed, from multiplication to division to subtraction to addition. If the math problem is answered correctly the box will fill with a checkmark, but if answered incorrectly the box will fill with a X. If 5 math problems are answered correctly in a row, either vertically, horizontally, or diagnolly. You Win!
